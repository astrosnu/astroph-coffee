Welcome to Astro-Coffee@SNU!

{% end %} {% block pagecontent %}

Every Tuesday, the astro folks at Seoul National University gathers in Bldg. 19's Rm. 206 to discuss the latest arXiv astro-ph posting at 10:00 AM over coffee. This astro-coffee server is meant to help organize the discussion. It automatically finds and highlights papers from local authors, and allows the community to vote for articles they want to talk about. New paper listings are fetched from the arXiv between 00:25 and 00:35 UTC Monday through Friday during the week.

Currently, voting on papers is open between 12:00 AM before and 09:00 AM the morning of astroph meeting. Everyone gets five votes (one per paper) to use as they please.
